## Clarifications

### Session 2026-05-10

- Q: When admin clicks the «اعتماد التسوية» or «تعليم كمدفوعة» buttons in the action bar on `pages/admin/teacher-settlement-details.html`, what UI feedback should occur? → A: **Confirmation modal**. Clicking opens a sanctioned modal (Constitution V #4 — modals are one of the six allowed JS interactions) reusing the existing `assets/js/main.js` modal handler. The modal shows the projected post-action state (e.g., «التسوية ستنتقل إلى حالة معتمدة — السداد المتوقّع 2026-05-15» for «اعتماد التسوية»; «ستُسجَّل الدفعة بمبلغ 3,750.00 ريال إلى الحساب SA03********0042 بتاريخ 2026-05-10» for «تعليم كمدفوعة») plus secondary captions clarifying that this is a visual-only demo (no real payment will be issued). Modals expose «تأكيد» (primary) + «إلغاء» (secondary) buttons per Constitution §Visual System. Zero new JS introduced; the markup reuses the existing modal pattern from prior specs.
- Q: The April 2026 settlement carries a +200.00 ريال «حافز التميز» tied to the locked Spec-006 92% attendance figure. Should the spec include a documented threshold rule (so teachers and admins can see WHY this bonus was granted)? → A: **Documented threshold caption**. A small «سياسة حافز التميز» caption MUST appear on both `pages/teacher/settlement-details.html` (read-only) and `pages/admin/teacher-settlement-details.html` (admin-editable in a future spec, read-only here) explaining the trigger rule: «يُمنَح عند تجاوز نسبة حضور الطلاب 90% خلال الشهر + تقييم إيجابي من المراقب الأكاديمي — المبلغ 200.00 ريال ثابت». The April figure 92% (locked in Spec 006) MUST be cited verbatim alongside the rule so the cause-effect is explicit. The spec deliberately commits to a single threshold (90%) + a fixed amount (200) — not a tiered scale — to avoid overcommitting the academy to a compensation policy the user did not request.
- Q: When admin edits/creates a salary rule on `pages/admin/teacher-salary-rules.html`, the rule applies forward-only — prior settlements are NOT recalculated. Should the form make this retroactivity rule explicit? → A: **Visible callout on form + table footnote**. A permanent callout MUST render directly above (or beside) the rule-form's effective-date input with the text: «تسري القاعدة على التسويات الجديدة فقط من تاريخ السريان — لا تُعدَّل التسويات السابقة أو المدفوعة أو المعتمدة. لتعديل تسوية مغلقة، يُرجى استخدام «إعادة فتح التسوية» في صفحة تفاصيل التسوية الإدارية.» Additionally, a small footnote MUST appear below the rules table itself: «* جميع القواعد تُطبَّق إلى الأمام من تاريخ السريان.» This mirrors Spec 008's FR-088 tax-policy regulatory-transparency pattern and makes the no-retroactive-recalculation rule visible to any stakeholder demoing the page — eliminating the risk of a misinterpretation when an admin raises a rate.
- Q: How should the 9 in-progress May sessions on `pages/teacher/earnings.html` decompose across the 4 rate buckets? → A: **Decomposed into 4 buckets with revised total**. The May running-tally row breaks down as: 5 × 30.00 (ناطق-جماعية = 150.00) + 2 × 60.00 (ناطق-خاصة = 120.00) + 1 × 50.00 (غير-ناطق-جماعية = 50.00) + 1 × 100.00 (غير-ناطق-خاصة = 100.00) = **9 sessions, 420.00 ريال** (revised down from the original ~720.00 estimate, which was arithmetically inconsistent with the persona's blended per-session rates). The earnings-history table May row MUST display the 420.00 figure with status «قيد التحصيل» and a small caption «تفصيل: 5+2+1+1 جلسة عبر 4 أنواع» making the bucket breakdown traceable. The "ملخص قواعد الراتب الفعّالة" side panel (FR-018) covers the rules; the May row's caption confirms which buckets the running-tally figures came from. This preserves the fidelity of the Arabic-vs-Foreign rate distinction even on partial-month rows.

### User Story 3 - Admin defines salary rules and disburses an advance (Priority: P3)

A platform-settings admin opens "قواعد رواتب المعلمين" (`teacher-salary-rules.html`) once a quarter to update per-teacher pricing — for example, raising الأستاذ أحمد's Arabic-private rate from 60.00 ريال to 65.00 ريال effective 2026-06-01. The admin scrolls to the rule-creation/edit form panel below the table, picks the teacher, picks the course, picks the student-type ("ناطق بالعربية"), chooses the rate-type ("لكل حصة خاصة"), enters the amount 65.00 + currency ريال, picks the effective date 2026-06-01, adds a note, and clicks the visual "حفظ القاعدة" button. Separately the admin opens "إنشاء سلفة" (`create-teacher-advance.html`) to disburse a pre-approved 800-ريال advance to الأستاذة منى سعد (TCH-2024-0051) — selecting teacher, entering amount 800.00, picking reason "علاج طبي طارئ" from the dropdown, picking deduction method "تقسيط شهري ثابت" with 4 monthly installments of 200, picking deduction-start month "يونيو 2026", attaching an admin note, and clicking the visual "حفظ السلفة" button. A "بعد الحفظ" caption near the primary button explains the new advance will appear on `pages/admin/teacher-advances.html` and on الأستاذة منى's `pages/teacher/advances.html` — informational only, no JS handler.

**Why this priority**: These two pages (`teacher-salary-rules`, `create-teacher-advance`) are configuration / back-office tools used less frequently than the operational pages in Story 2. ### Edge Cases

- **Settlement-state ladder**: the spec deliberately renders the SAME anchor settlement (April 2026, الأستاذ أحمد) in the **بانتظار الاعتماد** state (today 2026-05-10), with the prior month (March 2026, 3,400.00 ريال) in **مدفوعة** terminal state on the same earnings table. This dual rendering MUST be visible — it demonstrates BOTH the live-action affordance (admin's approve/mark-paid CTAs are clickable) AND the closed-state visual treatment (download-only). A page that shows EITHER all-paid OR all-pending fails this gate.
- **Advance double-counting prevention**: the April settlement deducts ADV-2026-0012's *final* installment (200 ريال — month 3 of 3) AND ADV-2026-0023's *first* installment (250 ريال — month 1 of 4) for a total of 450 ريال. The advances-page table MUST show ADV-2026-0012 as "شبه مكتملة" (status reads "آخر دفعة ضمن تسوية أبريل المعلّقة") rather than "مكتملة" — because until the April settlement is approved + paid, the final 200 has not been applied. Once status flips to مدفوعة (in a hypothetical future state, not visible today), the row would flip to مكتملة. This dependency between settlement state and advance state MUST be visibly captioned on the advance row.
- **Multi-currency teacher in admin tables**: at least one EGP-denominated teacher (الأستاذة هبة عبد الفتاح — Cairo) coexists in admin/teacher-finance.html, admin/teacher-advances.html, and admin/teacher-salary-rules.html alongside 5 SAR teachers. The currency tag MUST be a visible badge per row (not just a tooltip) and totals/sums MUST NOT mix currencies across rows in any summary tile (admin/teacher-finance.html exposes separate "إجمالي المستحقات بالريال" and "إجمالي المستحقات بالجنيه" tiles).
- **Read-only enforcement on teacher pages**: the teacher (الأستاذ أحمد) sees his earnings, advances, and settlement details — but **CANNOT** edit any value. There are no input fields on `pages/teacher/earnings.html`, `pages/teacher/advances.html`, or `pages/teacher/settlement-details.html` that accept teacher-edited financial values (only filter affordances + download buttons). A "ملاحظة الشفافية" caption near each page's hero explicitly states "العرض للقراءة فقط — لتعديل أي قيمة، يُرجى التواصل مع الإدارة".
- **Bonuses panel visibility**: not every settlement carries a bonus. The April settlement carries a +200.00 ريال "حافز التميز" entry; the March settlement on the earnings-history table did NOT — its bonus column shows "—". The bonuses-visual section on settlement-details MUST gracefully render the empty state when bonus = 0 (a single neutral row "لا توجد حوافز هذا الشهر") rather than collapsing the panel.
- **Manual adjustment with negative amount**: the April settlement has ONE manual deduction (-80.00 ريال — "خصم بسبب جلستين متأخرتين 14 + 22 أبريل") created by admin SARA.M on 2026-05-02. The admin/teacher-settlement-details.html manual-adjustments panel MUST visibly distinguish positive adjustments (success-tone +) from negative adjustments (danger-tone −) — color is not the sole indicator (sign + label both visible).
- **Course-percentage rule with zero course revenue**: the salary-rules table contains at least one rule with a defined course-percentage (e.g., الأستاذة منى سعد — لغة عربية — 18%) where the underlying course generated 0 ريال in the rendered month. The admin/teacher-finance.html row for that teacher MUST show 0 in the course-earnings column — not omit it — and the settlement-details page (if visited) MUST list the course-percentage rule with a "0.00 (لم تُسجَّل إيرادات هذا الشهر)" caption rather than hide it.
- **Empty-advance state (NOT applicable to anchor teacher)**: الأستاذ أحمد has 3 advances on file. A reference teacher (الأستاذ خالد العبدلي — Islamic Studies) has 0 advances on file — this is reflected on admin/teacher-advances.html as zero rows associated with him AND on admin/teacher-finance.html as a "—" in the "السلف المخصومة" column. The empty-state rendering on the **teacher-side** advances page (if a teacher with 0 advances opens it) is documented in FR-022 but not separately rendered (the spec's anchor teacher has 3 advances).
- **Pending-advance request**: at least one row on admin/teacher-advances.html has status "بانتظار الاعتماد" (e.g., ADV-2026-0031 — الأستاذ يوسف القحطاني — 500 ريال — pending since 2026-05-08). Rejected advances MUST remain visible in the table (not deleted) for audit-trail transparency; they MUST visually be greyed-out or carry a "محفوظ للسجل" caption.
- **In-progress May earnings disclaimer**: the May 2026 row on the earnings-history table is partial (today 2026-05-10 — only 9 sessions logged so far → 420.00 ريال running tally, decomposed across 4 rate buckets per Q4: 5 ناطق-جماعية + 2 ناطق-خاصة + 1 غير-ناطق-جماعية + 1 غير-ناطق-خاصة). ### Key Entities

- **Teacher**: A staff member who delivers sessions and courses. Attributes: teacher ID (e.g., `TCH-2024-0042`), full name, subject/specialization, hire date, contract type (عقد دائم / عقد بالحصة), nationality, payroll currency (SAR or EGP), bank account (bank name + masked IBAN), active status. The anchor teacher الأستاذ أحمد بن عبد الله الزهراني (TCH-2024-0042) is locked across this spec and Spec 002.
- **Salary Rule**: A pricing rule binding a teacher to a course with one or more rate components. Attributes: rule ID, teacher, course, student-type (ناطق بالعربية / غير ناطق بالعربية / كلاهما), rate components (any non-empty subset of: per-Arabic-group rate, per-Arabic-private rate, per-Foreign-group rate, per-Foreign-private rate, course-revenue percentage, fixed monthly amount), currency, effective date, status (نشطة / موقوفة), notes, last-edit author + timestamp.
- **Session-Earning Bucket**: A grouping of completed sessions within a settlement period that share both student-type and group/private mode. Four canonical buckets per teacher per month: (Arabic-group, Arabic-private, Foreign-group, Foreign-private). Attributes: bucket key, session count, per-session rate, bucket subtotal.
- **Course Earning**: A monthly payout component derived from a course's revenue × the teacher's percentage rule (or the teacher's fixed monthly amount). Attributes: course, period, course-revenue, teacher-percentage, teacher-share, OR fixed monthly amount.
- **Bonus**: A discretionary positive adjustment to a settlement. Attributes: label, amount, granted-by author, granted-at date, reason. Multiple bonuses MAY exist on a single settlement (the anchor April settlement carries one).
- **Manual Deduction / Adjustment**: An admin-recorded line item on a settlement (positive or negative). Attributes: label, signed amount, author, date, reason. Distinguished visually from advance deductions (a system-generated category) and bonuses (positive-only category).
- **Advance**: A money-out event from the academy to the teacher to be deducted from future settlements. Attributes: advance ID (e.g., `ADV-2026-0023`, EGP variant `ADV-2026-EG-0007`), teacher, granted date, amount, currency, reason, deduction method (تقسيط شهري ثابت / دفعة واحدة / نسبة من التسوية), installments-count (when applicable), deduction-start month, status (نشطة / مكتملة / شبه مكتملة / بانتظار الاعتماد / مرفوضة), admin notes thread.
- **Advance Installment Event**: A single deduction occurrence applied to one settlement. Attributes: advance ID, installment ordinal (e.g., 1 of 4), settlement period, amount deducted. The April 2026 settlement of الأستاذ أحمد contains two such events: ADV-2026-0012 ordinal 3/3 (-200) and ADV-2026-0023 ordinal 1/4 (-250).
- **Settlement**: A monthly payroll record per teacher. Attributes: settlement ID (e.g., `STL-2026-04-TCH-0042`), teacher, period (month boundaries), session-earnings buckets (the four), course-earnings, bonuses (0..N), manual adjustments (0..N), advance deductions (0..N), gross subtotal, net payable, currency, status (مسودة / بانتظار الاعتماد / معتمدة / مدفوعة / مرفوضة), expected approval date, expected payment date, target bank account snapshot, audit-trail timeline.
- **Settlement Status Ladder**: The five workflow states a settlement transitions through: مسودة (system-generated) → بانتظار الاعتماد (after admin review) → معتمدة (after director approval) → مدفوعة (after bank transfer) — with مرفوضة as a side-branch from any pre-paid state. The April anchor settlement is rendered in بانتظار الاعتماد; the March settlement of الأستاذ أحمد and the April settlement of الأستاذ خالد are rendered in مدفوعة; the April settlements of الأستاذة منى + الأستاذة هبة are rendered in معتمدة (in-flight, not yet paid).
